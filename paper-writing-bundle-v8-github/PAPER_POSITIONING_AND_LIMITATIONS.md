# Paper Positioning and Limitations

## Recommended title direction

Preferred title:
A Trustworthy Federated Hybrid Digital Twin for Resilient Renewable Energy Management in Grid-Interactive Virtual Power Plants

## Main emphasis

Focus on:
- hybrid twin for observability and calibration
- personalized federated and hybrid forecasting
- trust-aware risk-scheduled MPC
- cost and resilience gains under abnormal conditions
- deployment-oriented, non-deep, computationally practical design

## Claims to keep modest

Avoid making the headline about:
- deep MARL
- unconstrained RL comparisons
- dramatic peak shaving everywhere
- universally beneficial uncertainty modeling

## Items that should be acknowledged clearly

State plainly that:
- the uncertainty ablation is mixed in the final results
- peak-import gains are scenario-dependent and usually modest
- trust-aware control often increases battery throughput
- the evaluation is benchmark-based rather than hardware-in-the-loop
- the market design and tariff assumptions are experiment-specific
